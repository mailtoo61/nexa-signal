# Preview Video Shotlist

Target length: 15-30 seconds.
Goal: communicate calm tactical depth, clean recovery, and polished controls.

## 1) Opening Hook (0:00-0:03)

- Show Home state with strong visual clarity.
- Transition into Enter Network action.
- Message intent: immediate understanding of premium systems strategy tone.

## 2) Home Flow (0:03-0:06)

- Briefly show mode entry and composure of UI.
- Keep transitions smooth and minimal.

## 3) Gameplay Flow (0:06-0:14)

- Show stabilize/repair/reroute sequence.
- Include one moment of rising pressure.
- Keep the sequence truthful to real input pacing.

## 4) Collapse / Recovery Tension Moment (0:14-0:19)

- Show near-failure or collapse summary state.
- Immediately show Continue Network context or clean restart path.
- Message intent: consequence + reliable recovery.

## 5) Accessibility / Settings Moment (0:19-0:23)

- Briefly show settings controls:
  - reduced motion
  - audio
  - haptics
  - language

## 6) Ending CTA Flow (0:23-0:30)

- Return to strong gameplay or Home identity frame.
- End on title presence and calm confidence.
- No exaggerated claims, no fake urgency text.

## Timing Notes

- Avoid rapid cuts under ~1 second unless needed for readability.
- Prioritize readable UI over flashy transition density.

## Capture Cleanliness Rules

- Production-visible UI only.
- No dev panels or debug labels.
- No fake telemetry overlays.
- No mocked gameplay events that cannot occur naturally.
- Keep touch cadence human and believable.

## Audio Notes (Optional)

- Use in-game feel where possible.
- Keep mix balanced; no clipping or aggressive compression.
