## Long Description

`NEXA SIGNAL` is a modern systems strategy game built around clarity, tension, and control.

You guide a living network under pressure: stabilizing overloaded nodes, repairing weak links, and rerouting flow before collapse cascades through the grid.

Sessions are fast, readable, and replayable. Decision quality matters more than speed, and each run rewards calm tactical planning.

The experience is designed for production-grade mobile play:

- Deterministic resume and recovery behavior.
- Local-first progress with no account requirement for core play.
- Accessibility-aware controls including reduced motion and feedback settings.
- Language support prepared for global release flows.

Whether you have two minutes or twenty, `NEXA SIGNAL` is built to feel precise, composed, and satisfying from first launch to long-term mastery.

## Store Positioning Notes

- Core positioning: premium-feeling systems strategy with deterministic reliability.
- Player promise: clear decisions, controlled pressure, and respectful UX.
- Avoid in marketing copy: exaggerated claims, competitive superlatives, or unverified performance/legal statements.

## Onboarding / Value Messaging

- First-session message: approachable entry, immediately readable decisions.
- Mid-session message: tension rises through network instability, not visual noise.
- Long-term message: mastery comes from consistent tactical discipline and recovery-aware play.

## Accessibility Messaging (Store-Safe)

- Include mention of reduced motion controls.
- Include mention of adjustable audio/haptics preferences.
- Avoid claiming complete accessibility compliance until formally validated.
